Carrot Top

Using AR technique(HoloLens) to tell people when to harvest

n the 48 hour-long AT&T AR/VR hackathon, my team created an AR application CarrotTop for Microsoft HoloLens. CarrotTop used the built-in camera on HoloLens to detect and predict the length of the carrot that buried underground with the shape of the leaf. Combining the technique of image recognition and augmented reality, CarrotTop overlays a holographic on top of the real carrot to provide the optimal harvest information which helps the farmer or amateurs harvest the carrot at the right time.
